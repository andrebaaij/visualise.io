Templates!

Should everybody know how to code interactive visualisations? Not in our opinion. We have taken a different approach where we supply you with templates that you can easily customise with your own data and styling! Is there no template suited for your needs? Just create your own!

Backend

Our backend uses D3.js, this is a JavaScript library for manipulating documents based on data. It gives us the full capabilities of modern browsers. Don't worry, you won't need to write anything while creating visualisations.

Interactive

A lot of the templates are optimized for onscreen interactions. This means awesome visualisations! Take a look at the examples, or go and create your own.
